# 📊 Global Electronics Sales Performance Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents an interactive sales performance dashboard built using **Tableau Public**, analyzing global electronics retail data across 9 countries from 2016 to 2021. The dashboard is designed to provide actionable business insights for sales performance monitoring, product analysis, and regional market evaluation.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze overall sales performance including total revenue, total orders, total customers, and average order value
- Identify top performing products and categories driving the majority of revenue
- Evaluate regional sales distribution across 9 countries
- Monitor customer retention through repeat purchase rate analysis
- Identify revenue trends and seasonal patterns from 2016 to 2021

---

## 📁 Dataset
| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Source** | Maven Analytics — Global Electronics Retailer Dataset |
| **Period** | 2016 — 2021 |
| **Countries** | 9 Countries |
| **Link** | [Maven Analytics Data Playground](https://mavenanalytics.io/data-playground) |

### Tables:
| Table | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| `Sales.csv` | Transaction records including order date, quantity, and currency |
| `Products.csv` | Product details including name, brand, category, unit cost, and unit price |
| `Customers.csv` | Customer demographics including gender, location, and birthday |
| `Stores.csv` | Store details including country, state, and square meters |
| `Exchange_Rates.csv` | Daily currency exchange rates |

## 📈 Dashboard Features
- ✅ **5 KPI Cards** — Total Revenue, Total Orders, Repeat Rate, Total Customers, AOV with YoY trend indicators
- ✅ **Revenue Trend by Year** — Line chart with trendline showing revenue pattern from 2016 to 2021
- ✅ **Revenue by Country** — Bar chart showing sales distribution across 9 countries
- ✅ **Revenue by Category** — Bar chart showing performance across 8 product categories
- ✅ **Top 10 Products by Revenue** — Horizontal bar chart showing best selling products
- ✅ **Interactive Filters** — Select Year, Select Category, Select Country

---

## 💡 Key Insights
> 🇺🇸 **United States** dominates revenue at **$24M**, followed by Online channel at **$11M**

> 💻 **Computers** is the top performing category at **$19M**, representing the largest share of total revenue

> 📈 **Peak revenue** of **$2.47M** was recorded in **2019**

> 🔄 **61.18% repeat purchase rate** indicates strong customer loyalty

> 📉 Significant decline in 2021 metrics reflects **incomplete data for that year (partial year)**, not actual business performance

---

## ⚠️ Data Notes
- All revenue figures are presented in **USD**
- The significant decline in 2021 KPI metrics reflects incomplete data for that year (partial year), not actual business performance
- `Delivery Date` column in Sales table contains approximately 75% null values and was excluded from analysis
